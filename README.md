## ⌁ About

<img align="right" width="300" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DenverCoder1/readme-typing-svg/main/img/animated-emoji.gif" width="0" height="0"/>

I'm anxnt — a cybersecurity and software engineer who lives at the point where the two disciplines collide: building systems, then trying to break them, then building them again so they don't break the same way twice.

Outside of code and terminals, I write. My most recent work, **Restoration Asymmetry**, explores the idea that recovery — of systems, of people, of anything — is never a mirror of the damage that caused it. It's a theme that shows up in my security research too: breach and recovery are rarely symmetric.

> engineer, researcher, author — in that order some days, reversed on others
